About Me

Christopher Chorney is a cello player and multi-instrumentalist who has played on numerous Grammy-Award Winning tracks with such artists as Kanye West and Jay-Z. He plays the cello solo at the start of the song All Of The Lights, which has garnered over 250 million views on YouTube. He is the owner of Analog Rock Machine Studio in Honolulu, Hawaii which has produced numerous albums for a diverse array of local musicians. He currently resides in Richmond Virginia.
